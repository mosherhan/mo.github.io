Hey! I am Mohammed Sherhan and Welcome to my portfolio! Building digital products and electronics systems has been one of my favorite passions since 2020. This website is solely based on user experience, refined aesthetics, and immersive design.

As the co-founder of MakeWithUs, I specialize in full-stack web development, UI/UX, and product strategy. I also lead Bichikat, a luxury fashion brand, where I design premium visuals, manage branding, and develop seamless eCommerce experiences. I have a strong foundation in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Tailwind, and I actively explore advanced design tools and frameworks. I come from an Electronics Engineering background with a focus on audio electronics, sound design, and system-level prototyping. I am passionate about merging physical tech with digital interaction. Whether working independently or with a team, I stay committed to delivering innovative and polished results in every project I work on.
